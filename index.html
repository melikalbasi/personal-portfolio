About Me

I'm Melika Kalbasi, a conceptual art director and web developer. I believe in creating things that not only function seamlessly, but are also aesthetically pleasing.

I'm a California native, currently residing in San Francisco. I graduated from San Francisco State with my Bachelor's in Communication Studies and continued my education at the UC Berkeley Extension Coding Bootcamp where I furthered my knowledge in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
